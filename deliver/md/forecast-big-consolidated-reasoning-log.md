2. **Active voice** — "คุณสามารถทำได้" not "สามารถทำได้โดยคุณ"
3. **Concrete before abstract** — Open with everyday meaning, then unfold deeper layer
4. **Layered depth** — Reader can stop after first paragraph and still get value
